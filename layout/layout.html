LAYOUT

Third floor

Stack Area - It houses subject-wise text-books and other books arranged on open racks for circulation among students.

Circulation Desk - All the issue-return transactions take place here. Reference query, issue of library membership card, clearance, late-fine etc. are all dealt at this place.

OPAC Terminal - Each floor of the library has one computer system connected on library intranet from which library database of books and journals can be browsed and searched through the library client software. Students can use this terminal to look into their library account, late-fine accrued, and for making their entire search query with respect to titles, copies & their locations, new arrivals etc.

Processing Area - This area is exclusively meant for library staff from where all the back- end operations are done such as acquisition of books, processing of books and other administrative work.

Fourth floor

Reference Collection - It is comprised primarily of reference books and single copy of text-books arranged subject-wise in book- cases kept under closed access.

Computer Hub - The library also houses a centralized computing infrastructure comprising of a network of 21 PC connected to University of Delhi through optical fiber link. Students can browse Internet and access all the e-resources such as e-books, e-journals, other databases subscribed by the University of Delhi. Students can use this facility for preparing their assignments/ project report etc. Print-outs are available to students on payment @ Re. 1.00 per page through POS machine.

Specialized reading services for Visually Challenged - It has Document capture scanner with screen reading software.

Fifth floor

Reading Hall - It has capacity to accommodate around 200 students in the seating area.

Text-book Consultation - This section has essential prescribed text-books for consultation by students.

Periodical/ Magazine & Newspaper Display - The racks display magazines & journals subscribed by the library. These are arranged alphabetically for easy retrieval. As many as 15 prominent national dailies are also available here.
