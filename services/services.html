Services

Bibliography Services
The library prepares subject-wise bibliography of books on a specific topic of interest as per the requirement of user.
Current Awareness Services
Through this service users are made aware about new books which arrive in library on quarterly basis.
Book Bank Service
This service is made available to students belonging to SC/ST/OBC and other economically weaker sections. This entitles the student to borrow 3 to 4 books of their choice available in the book bank collection for the entire duration of a semester or whole year. Application for this privilege is normally invited in the last week of September every year.
Database/ Article Search
The library performs article search on subscribed e-journals databases and other e-resources available in the public domain on a specific topic of interest. The search results along with the available full text are given to the members in soft copy form.
Print-Out Services
All the members can utilize print-out services through Central Computing facility of the library. This service is chargeable @ Re. 1.00 per page. The payment made through POS machine.
Inter Library Loan (ILL)
The library makes all efforts to arrange and augment its collection through ILL.
